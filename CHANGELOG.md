## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-25

Initial public release.

### Added

- Interactive emergency-zone map with a structural-status traffic light (collapsed, damaged, stable, unconfirmed), always shown as color plus icon plus text label.
- Zone reporting via geolocation or by picking a point on the map, including photo uploads restricted to safe raster image types.
- Needs per zone (rescue, water, food, medicine, shelter, and more) with a needed, on-the-way, covered lifecycle.
- Verified emergency phone directory by state with tap-to-call and the source of each number.
- Aid guide covering what to donate and what to avoid, shelters and collection centers, and aid organizations.
- Link to the sister project for missing persons.
- Live updates on the map and lists via Supabase Realtime.
- PWA support with light and dark mode and a mobile-first design.
- SEO and Open Graph metadata.
